TTBA SYMPOSIUM

The first TTBA symposium will be held on November 21st, in the heart of Texas at the Baylor College of Medicine Medical Center in Houston, TX.

Dr. Shu Chien (錢煦) and Dr. Michael Chang (張念慈) will give keynote sessions covering bioengineering, medicine, entrepreneurship, and cancer research. (Dr. Mien-Chie Huang has a schedule conflict.) Panel sessions with young professionals from academia and industry will be held where panelists will discuss their experience and career options. Submit your abstract for the poster session, where speakers, panelists and fellow attendees will gather to network and exchange ideas.

We have proudly organized the TTBA symposium for you, for Taiwan, and for the worldwide biotechnology community. We look forward to seeing you in Houston.

Dr. Michael Chang 張念慈
Dr. Shu Chien 錢煦
