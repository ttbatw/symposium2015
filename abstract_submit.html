Poster Session

The poster session is the event to network and meet potential collaborators, mentors and employers. As it is said (by scientists), a figure is worth a thousand words, and we highly encourage you to bring a research poster to foster discussion, brainstorming, and exchange of ideas with fellow symposium attendees.

Please use the Abstract Template, read the guidelines below, and upload or email your abstract in .docx or .doc format.

We will review all submissions and select 10 attendees to give a 5-minute elevator talk and promote your posters.
